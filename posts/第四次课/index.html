Duhamel原理

设$w=w(x,t,\tau)$是初值问题 $$ \begin{cases} \frac{\partial^2 w}{\partial t^2}=a^2\frac{\partial^2 w}{\partial x^2},0\leqslant x\leqslant l,0\leqslant t\leqslant T\ w|{t=\tau}=0,\frac{\partial w}{\partial t}|{t=\tau}=f(x,\tau),0\leqslant x\leqslant l,\tau\leqslant t\leqslant T

\end{cases} $$ 的两次连续可微解,则 $$ \frac{\partial}{\partial t}u(x,t)=w(x,t,\tau)+\int_{0}^{t}\frac{\partial }{\partial t}w(x,t,\tau)d\tau=\int_{0}^{t}\frac{\partial }{\partial t}w(x,t,\tau)d\tau $$ 于是$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t}|{t=0}=0$,且 $$ \frac{\partial ^2}{\partial t^2}u(x,t)=\int{0}^{t}\frac{\partial^2 }{\partial t^2}w(x,t,\tau)d\tau+\frac{\partial w}{\partial t}|{t=t}=a^2 \int{0}^{t}\frac{\partial^2 }{\partial x^2}w(x,t,\tau)d\tau+f(x,t)=a^2\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}+f(x,t) $$

则$u(x,t)=\int_{0}^{t}w(x,t,\tau)d\tau$是初值问题 $$ \begin{cases} \frac{\partial^2 w}{\partial t^2}=a^2\frac{\partial^2 w}{\partial x^2},0\leqslant x\leqslant l,0\leqslant t\leqslant T\ w|{t=\tau}=0,\frac{\partial w}{\partial t}|{t=\tau}=f(x,\tau),0\leqslant x\leqslant l,\tau\leqslant t\leqslant T

\end{cases} $$ 的解.
Duhamel 原理又称为外力化初速度原理。应用 Duhamel 原理能把非齐次发展方程的定解问题，化归为齐次发展方程的定解问题。

无界弦的自由振动

无界弦的自由振动归结为初值问题 $$ \begin{cases} \frac{\partial ^2 u}{\partial t^2}=a^2\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2},(x\in R,t> 0)\ u|{t=0}=\varphi(x),\frac{\partial u}{\partial t}|{t=0}=\psi(x),x\in R

\end{cases} $$

特征方程$L_u=A\frac{(\partial ^2 u)}{\partial x^2}+B\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x\partial y}+C\frac{\partial ^2 u}{\partial y^2}+Du+E=0$
可以有F(x,y)=$A(dy)^2-2Bdxdy+C(dx)^2$

$\frac{\partial ^2 u}{\partial t^2}-a^2\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}=0即(dx)^2-a^2(dt)^2=0$

找$\Delta$和0的比较发现是双曲线.

又可以得到:$(dx-adt)(dx+adt)=0\Rightarrow \begin{cases} dx=adt\ dx=-adt \end{cases} \Rightarrow \begin{cases} x=at+c\x=-at+c\end{cases}$
令$\begin{cases}\xi =x-at\ \eta=x+at\end{cases}$
将$\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2},\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial t^2}$的计算式以上方程代入得 $$ \frac{\partial ^2 u}{\partial \xi \partial \eta}=0 $$ (为什么是0看第三次课最后)

$\frac{\partial}{\partial \eta}(\frac{\partial v}{\partial \xi})=0 \Rightarrow \frac{\partial v}{\partial \xi}=f’1(\xi)$
$v=\int f’1(\xi )d\xi+f_2(\eta)$
$v(\xi,\eta)=f_1(\xi)+f_2(\eta)=f_1(x-at)+f_2(x+at)=u(x,t)$ $$ \begin{cases} u|{t=0}=f_1(x)+f_2(x)=\varphi(x)\ \frac{\partial u}{\partial t}|{t=0}=-af’1(x-at)+af’2(x+at)|{t=0}=-af_1’(x)+af’2(x)=\psi(x) \end{cases} $$ 对以上计算得,
$$ \begin{cases} f_1(x)=\frac{1}{2}\varphi(x)-\frac{1}{2a}\int{x_0}^{x}\psi(x)dx-\frac{c}{2}\ f_2(x)=\frac{1}{2}\varphi(x)+\frac{1}{2a}\int{x_0}^{x}\psi(s)ds+\frac{c}{2}\ \end{cases} $$ 代入$u(x,t)=f_1(x-at)+f_2(x+at)$得通解 $$ u(x,t)=\frac{1}{2}\varphi(x-at)+\frac{1}{2}\varphi(x+at)+\frac{1}{2a}\int_{x-at}^{x+at}\psi(\xi)d\xi $$
